A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam popularly known as Missile Man of India, was a visionary scientist, educator and statesman who left an indelible mark on India's space and missile programs.

Biographies

  • Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (15 October 1931 to 27 July 2015)
  • Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007
  • Born and raised in a Muslim family in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • He studied physics and aerospace engineering
  • Kalam was elected as the 11th president of India in 2002
